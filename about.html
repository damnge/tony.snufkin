ABOUT ME

I’m a photographer with a broad interest in anything associated with visual art. I'm currently based in Reykjavik, Iceland.

My deep interest in history and geography ignited my desire to travel. Traveling and exploring sparked a profound passion for photography, storytelling, and documentation. That’s why I’m so drawn to candid street photography — where nothing is staged, and everything unfolds naturally in its own unique way. During my travels, I’ve had the opportunity to shoot on the streets of some of the world's largest cities, including Tehran, Bangkok, Dubai, Sydney, Melbourne, São Paulo, and many more. Lately, my passion for digital photography has evolved into a love for film photography. Shooting on film has given me a completely different perspective on the art of photography.

If you wish to collaborate or if you are interested in prints, feel free to contact me.
